User Guide

Everything you need to get the best results from DermaRash.

Medical Disclaimer: DermaRash is an informational tool powered by AI. It is NOT a medical diagnostic device and does not replace consultation with a qualified dermatologist or healthcare professional. Always seek professional medical advice for any health concerns.

📸 Taking the Perfect Photo

  • Good lighting: Use natural daylight or a bright indoor light. Avoid harsh shadows or flash glare.
  • Close-up focus: Hold your phone 15–25 cm from the skin. The rash should fill most of the frame.
  • Sharp image: Tap your screen to focus before shooting. Avoid blurry or motion-blurred shots.
  • Clean skin: Remove any makeup, creams, or bandages from the area before scanning.
  • Multiple angles: If the rash is large or has distinct features, consider scanning different areas.
💡 Tip: The app automatically checks image quality and will alert you if the photo is too blurry, too dark, or too small for accurate analysis.

📝 Filling in the Details Form

Accurate symptom information significantly improves analysis quality. Required fields are marked with *.

  • Body Location* — Select the area of the body where the rash appears.
  • Duration* — How long have you had this condition? (Under 24 hours / 1–3 days / 1–2 weeks / Over 2 weeks)
  • Current Symptoms* — Check all that apply: redness, itching, swelling, warmth, pain, blistering, scaling, oozing.
  • First Appearance — Describe how the rash initially looked when it first appeared.
  • Possible Triggers — Note any potential triggers: new products, foods, stress, outdoor exposure, etc.
  • Recent Changes — Has the rash spread, changed color, or developed new features?
  • Current Medications — List any creams or treatments already applied.
⚠️ Important: If you are experiencing severe symptoms — widespread rash, difficulty breathing, high fever, or facial/throat swelling — call emergency services immediately. Do not use this app as a substitute for emergency care.

📄 Understanding Your Report

  • Possible Matches — Up to 3 potential skin conditions ranked by likelihood. Each includes why it may or may not match, and common signs.
  • Match Strength — Higher / Moderate / Lower — indicates how closely the visual and symptom data aligns with each condition.
  • Care Guidance — Practical steps you can take at home to manage symptoms.
  • Seek Care — Urgency level: Non-urgent review / Prompt review / Urgent — and specific reasons why you should consult a doctor.
  • Red Flags — Warning signs that require immediate medical attention.
💡 Tip: Save your report and show it to your doctor. It provides a structured summary that can help guide the consultation.

💬 Using the AI Chat

  • Tap the Chat tab to start a conversation with the AI skin-wellness assistant (educational only — not a medical consultation).
  • Ask follow-up questions about your scan results, general skin care, or symptoms.
  • You can attach photos directly in the chat for contextual analysis.
  • Free users receive 3 messages per day. Premium users have unlimited access.

💎 Free vs Premium

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

DermaRash ("we", "our", or "us"), operated by Evren Haznedaroglu, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the DermaRash mobile application.

⚕️

DermaRash processes skin photos solely to generate AI-assisted educational information for you. We do not sell or share your photos with any third party, and we do not use them to train AI models.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Skin photos: Images you capture or upload for analysis.
  • Symptom data: Body location, duration, symptoms, medications, and other health context you enter in the form.
  • Account information: Email address if you create an account (optional).
  • Chat messages: Text and images sent in the AI chat feature.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system version (for compatibility purposes).
  • App usage data (e.g., feature interactions) collected anonymously.
  • Subscription and purchase history managed by Apple/RevenueCat.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate your AI-assisted educational skin information report with citations.
  • To power the AI skin-wellness chat feature (educational responses only).
  • To maintain your scan history if you choose to save it.
  • To process and manage in-app subscriptions.
  • To respond to support requests.

We do NOT use your photos, symptom data, or chat messages to train or fine-tune AI models. The AI models (LLaVA and Llama 3.1) are open-source models that we run locally on our own servers; your data is used one time to produce your report and is not added to any training dataset.

3. Data Storage & Security

Skin photos you submit are processed on our own AI server (see Section 5). Images are not permanently stored unless you explicitly choose to save your scan history. All data transmissions between the app and our servers use HTTPS encryption. We implement industry-standard security measures including access controls and encrypted storage.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Apple Inc. — For in-app purchase and subscription management.
  • RevenueCat — For subscription analytics (anonymised).
  • Law enforcement — If required by law or legal process.

We do not share your skin photos or symptom data with any third parties. See Section 5 for full details on what is sent to our own AI server.

5. Data Sent to Our AI Service

When you tap Analyze, the DermaRash app transmits the following information directly to our own AI server (operated by the DermaRash team at derma.idakim.net) over an encrypted HTTPS connection:

  • The skin photo you captured or selected.
  • The body location you chose.
  • The duration of the condition you entered.
  • The symptoms and current medications you selected.
  • Any free-text notes you added (first appearance, triggers, recent changes).
  • Your selected app language (to localise the report).

Who receives this data. Only our own self-hosted DermaRash AI server receives this data. The server is operated by the DermaRash team and runs on infrastructure we control.

Third-party AI providers: none. DermaRash does NOT use OpenAI, Google (Gemini / Vertex AI), Anthropic, AWS Bedrock, Azure OpenAI, or any other third-party AI service. The underlying models — LLaVA (vision) and Llama 3.1 (text) — are open-source models running locally on our own servers. Your photos and symptom data are never transmitted to any third-party AI provider.

How the data is used. The photo and form fields are processed one time to generate your informational educational report with source citations. The data is not used to train AI models, not used for advertising, not sold, and not combined with any advertising or marketing profile.

Retention. If you save the scan to your history (Premium), the photo and report are retained only for as long as you keep them. You can delete any individual scan, clear your full scan history, or delete your entire account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Privacy & Data. If you do not save the scan, the photo is removed from our server after analysis.

Your control. You must explicitly grant data processing consent in the app before any scan can be submitted. You can revoke that consent at any time from Settings → Privacy Controls → Revoke Consent, which stops any further submissions.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You may request a copy of your stored data by contacting us.
  • Deletion: You may delete your scan history at any time from Settings → Delete History. To delete your account and all associated data, use Settings → Delete Account.
  • Consent Withdrawal: You may revoke your consent to data processing at any time from Settings → Privacy Controls.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the GDPR. If you are in California, you have rights under the CCPA. Contact us at info@idakimi.com to exercise any of these rights.

DermaRash is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Health-Related Imagery

DermaRash may process photos that contain sensitive health-related imagery. This data is used solely to generate your AI-assisted educational report and is handled with care. We are not a covered entity under HIPAA, and DermaRash is not a medical device under FDA, EU MDR, or UK MHRA rules. Reports are AI-generated educational information only and are not a diagnosis or medical advice.
